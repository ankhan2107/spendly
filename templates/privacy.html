Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any information beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service to you — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating your account.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable precautions to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Passwords are stored using one-way hashing and are never stored in plain text.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated expense data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your data with third-party analytics providers, advertisers, or data brokers. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface; this request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

5. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies or third-party cookies are set by Spendly. You can clear this cookie at any time by logging out or clearing your browser's cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will do our best to respond promptly.
